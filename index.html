Freifunk Darmstadt is a local Freifunk initiative, with the aim of providing a city-wide wireless network and free Internet access in Darmstadt.

Come to our next meeting: Mon, 5 May 2014, 7pm

The idea of Freifunk is to connect household WiFi routers to build an independent, city-wide network. Ideally, all nodes in the network can communicate with each other, even if there is no Internet access. In order to achieve this, we need the cooperation of the local people.

Everyone with a (compatible) wireless router can contribute to the local network, and even provide public wireless Internet access. This last step is optional, but is crucial to provide free Internet access to everyone. The more nodes connected to the Internet (exit nodes), the better is the Internet connection for everybody. You do not have to worry about sharing your Internet with the Freifunk users. To protect the Freifunk exit nodes, we route all Internet traffic through a VPN.

If you want to know more about Freifunk or collaborate with us,
you are very welcome to join us at our meetings.
